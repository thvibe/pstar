Nurse Work From Home

PatientStar is a specialized Contact Center Service Provider, with an immediate need and is seeking actively licensed RN's, LPN's, or Other Medical Professionals to fill the duty of a medical interviewer.

Duties Include:

  • Answer inbound calls from patients.
  • Read client-approved script to patients, gathering information
  • Enter answers to questions into computer system
  • Work as an independent contractor

Basic Requirements:

  • Minimum of a 2 year degree, prefer experience or degree in life sciences/healthcare. Ideal if you are a Nurse [RN/LPN/LVN], botech scientist, PharmD, Pharmacist, Doctor/MD, Foreign Medical Doctor, Physician Assistant, or similar medical license or education.
  • Prefer US based credentials, with at least two professional references (excluding relatives).
  • Must have basic computer skills.
  • Have a computer with high speed internet access (cable modem, DSL) (*Not reimbursed)
  • Have a traditional landline (No VOIP, Satellite or Magic Jack) (*Not reimbursed).

Hours of Operation / Shifts Available:

Daytime Hours: 8AM to 8PM [Eastern Standard Time]

The ability to work at least 15 to 20 hours per week (in 1 hour increments or greater) during some portion of the week (Monday - Friday) between the hours of 8am and 8pm Eastern Standard Time. Particular need for the hours 3pm to 8pm EST.

Daytime hours are open to RN's, LPN/LVN/Medical Professionals, Life Sciences Graduates.

After Hours: 8PM to 8AM [Eastern Standard Time], plus Weekends

The ability to work at least 20 to 25 hours per week during some portion of the following coverage times:

  • Monday - Friday between 8pm and 8am Eastern STandard Time
  • Saturday and Sunday (all day - midnight to 11:59pm)
  • After Hours are opened only to RNs or higher

This is an opportunity for someone that needs to work from home and who would like to expand their horizons with this growing business
